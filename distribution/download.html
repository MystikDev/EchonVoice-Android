Installing

  1. Tap Download Echon above.
  2. Open the downloaded file. Android will ask to allow installs from your browser — tap Settings and enable it, then go back.
  3. Tap Install, then Open.

Echon is signed by the Echon developer key. Future in-app updates are delivered automatically.
